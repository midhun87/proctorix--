Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

During the course of providing our proctoring services (Proctorix), we collect data necessary to ensure exam integrity. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Full name, email address, photo ID, and facial geometry data (biometrics) strictly for authentication.
  • Proctoring Data: Audio and video recordings, screen captures, keystroke patterns, and gaze-tracking data during the assessment period.
  • System Data: IP address, operating system, browser type, and running background processes (to detect unauthorized applications).

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected proctoring data is used exclusively to evaluate the integrity of the assessment. AI algorithms analyze video and audio streams to flag suspicious activities (e.g., multiple faces, unauthorized devices). Final reports are shared only with the assessing institution or employer.

3. Data Retention and Security

We employ enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit. Proctoring media (video/audio logs) are retained for a standard period of 90 days, or as mandated by the contracting institution, after which they are permanently deleted. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Proctorix Developer API

Integrate our AI proctoring and secure lockdown environments directly into your own HRMS or Assessment platform.

// Generate a new assessment session link
POST https://api.proctorix.in/v1/sessions
{ "candidate_email": "john@example.com", "assessment_id": "tech-frontend-001", "security_level": "strict", // Enables native lockdown app "webhook_url": "https://your-domain.com/webhooks/proctorix" }

Authentication

Authenticate your requests by including your secret API key in the Authorization header. You can manage your API keys in the Employer Dashboard.

API Reference

Detailed breakdown of all Proctorix endpoints, request payloads, and response structures.

GET /v1/candidates/{id}/report

Retrieves the full AI integrity report for a specific candidate session.

POST /v1/assessments/create

Creates a new assessment module dynamically from your HRMS.

Webhooks

Listen to real-time events triggered by candidates during examinations.

Available Events

  • session.started - Fired when a candidate passes identity verification.
  • violation.flagged - Fired when the AI detects a major integrity breach.
  • session.completed - Fired when the test is submitted.
